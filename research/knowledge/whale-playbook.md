# Whale Win-Back Playbook

_Synthesized 2026-07-04 from the OFM research corpus (152 competitor transcripts + consensus findings). Every play is cited. This feeds the whale-hunting page and the chatter briefs._

## The core belief (why this page exists)

> "It is actually easier to keep getting them to come back and spending more than it is to keep finding new viral reels to get people in the first place." — Markuss Hussle, *$56,500 from 5 fans in 30 days*

Whales are MADE by attention, not found: "A subscriber buys a $10 PPV and the chatter forgets about them. No follow-up, no tracking, no relationship — and that subscriber could have been a whale spending $500–$2,000/month if someone paid attention." — GriffinOFM, *10-hour course*

## Plays — urgent fans (falling off their rhythm)

1. **Never mass-message a whale.** High spenders get 1:1 personalized treatment only; top agencies explicitly EXCLUDE whales from smart/mass messages so every touch feels personal. (King Sam OFM — chatters exclude their whale lists from automations; consensus finding: "Give high spenders 1:1 personalized treatment, never mass messages")
2. **Voice notes are the strongest re-engagement touch.** Nurture big spenders with personal (client-voice) voice notes — "all they really care about is getting more content and feeling special." Use the creator's real voice memos for our whales. (Markuss Hussle masterclass — voice notes to whales "has been insane")
3. **Re-open with what HE is into, not a generic check-in.** Build/refresh the fan profile (relationship type, kinks, budget) and lead with his specific thing — the analysis brief exists for exactly this. (Habibi *A-Z chatting playbook* — "if it's 2025 and you're still saying 'babe'…"; consensus: "Tag every fan by relationship type and price accordingly")
4. **Games break fans out of a rut.** Spin-the-wheel / pick-a-number style games restarted stalled spenders before pitching anything. (Markuss Hussle masterclass)
5. **Price off his history.** When he re-engages, price PPVs from his own purchase history — not the menu. (consensus: "Price dynamically off purchase history")

## Plays — dormant whales & expired subs

6. **Message expired subscribers on day one.** The single most-repeated revival play: a discounted win-back mass message to ALL expired/lapsed subs — top agencies do this the day they take over an account. (consensus finding: "Win back expired subscribers with a discounted mass message on day one"; Markuss Hussle: "one of the first things you should do")
7. **Dormant WHALES get the 1:1 version.** For $500+ lifetime ghosts, the win-back is personal: reference something real from the old conversation ("you've missed so much…"), a renewed-sub promo, or a voice note — not the discount blast. (Habibi + King Sam)
8. **Rebill-off is the alarm before the silence.** Sub set to expire + rebill off = act before the expiry date, with a save offer. (our own live data confirms: the strongest churn predictor we've measured)

## Operating cadence (what the top agencies audit)

- On takeover/weekly: top fans, expired subs, who's currently subscribed, what content each whale bought and at what price. (Markuss Hussle masterclass — their "audit" = exactly this page)
- Track every buyer; follow up on every purchase — no orphaned $10 PPV buyers. (GriffinOFM)
- Watch chatting ratio (revenue : messages) — target 1:8–1:15. (GriffinOFM; consensus finding)
- Automate the generic re-engagement so chatters spend their time on inbound + whales. (consensus finding)
