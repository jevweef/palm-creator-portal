_Analyzed 2026-07-04 from the full archived chat (992 msgs, Jun 20 2025 → Jul 3 2026) +
2-year transaction history. The case that started the fan-intel system._

## Money shape

- Lifetime (our window): **$3,255 net** · Peak month **$624 (Feb 2025)** · Feb–Jun 2025 ≈ **$400–500/mo**
- Decay: Jul–Nov 2025 ($100–270) → Dec 2025–Apr 2026 ($0–80) → **revival sparks May–Jul 2026** ($185 / $21 / $90+)
- Price band: **$25–70 per unlock**, session buyer (3–4 unlocks a night, ~10pm–1am ET)
- Balks at: $80+ single items ("80 for 7 pics?!"), $145 ("too rich for tonight"), $70 (negotiated to $50)

## Who he is

Rapport whale. Calls her **"Queen."** Big Harry Potter fan, mountain climber, runs/bikes,
travels for work, Talking Heads is his favorite band, constant joker (Winnie-the-Pooh bit,
"you in the NBA now?"). Buys DURING live conversation, never from blasts.

## Engagement trigger: PARTICIPATION

His three most-engaged moments, all the same shape:
1. **Oct 22 2025 — Hermione session.** Content tied to HIS fandom → "I'm a big hp fan!!"
   → 4 PPVs bought in 40 min (~$148). "I'm Ron and my wand is ducking ready."
2. **Narration sessions (Sep 1 2025, Jun 1 2026).** When asked to describe what HE'd do,
   he writes paragraphs — and buys the ladder while doing it ($155 on Jun 1 2026).
3. **Dec 10 2025 — the banter on-ramp.** Chatter simply laughed at his joke; within four
   messages HE escalated to his sit-on-face fantasy unprompted. Humor → arousal → (missed) sale.

**The formula that worked every single time:** banter → his theme → he narrates → ladder $25–70.

## What killed him (prior agency era — Oct/Nov 2025)

1. **Broken $550 video-call promise** (Oct 22 2025; date set Nov 3; "I'll confirm" — never
   did; he chased Nov 7: "you never got back to me about the vc"). Trust broken + sale lost.
2. **Price-gouged at the ceiling** (Nov 7 2025): $125 spent, balked at $80, hit with $145
   → "Too rich for tonight" → chatter begged with a discount 90 min later. Last real session for months.
3. **Blast burial** (Nov 13 2025 → May 2026): ~150 mass PPVs, virtually all unopened,
   "daddy" spam-voice vs his "Queen" dynamic. Spending collapsed to $10–80/mo.
4. **Ignored buy-signals:** asked for solo/dildo-play videos 3× (Sep 25 2025, Dec 6 2025 —
   custom quoted $100/min, Dec 10 2025) — never followed up. That's a ~$300 sale still waiting.

## Proof of revival

Every time a chatter actually talked to him again, he bought immediately: Jun 1 2026
($41+$45+$69 all bought), Jul 2 2026 ($51+$61 bought after negotiating $70→$50).
The formula still works. The relationship survived the neglect.
